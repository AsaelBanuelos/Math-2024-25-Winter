## 1. Basic Operations on Matrices

For follwing matrices 

$$
\mathbf{A}=
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 2 \\
3 & 4 
\end{pmatrix}
\qquad
\mathbf{B}=
\begin{pmatrix}
5 & 6 \\
7 & 8
\end{pmatrix}
\quad
\mathbf{C}=
\begin{pmatrix}
-1 & 2 \\
3 & 0
\end{pmatrix}
\qquad
\mathbf{D}=
\begin{pmatrix}
-1 & 2 & 3 \\
4 & 0 & 6 
\end{pmatrix}
\qquad
\mathbf{E}=
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 2\\
4 & 5\\
7 & 8
\end{pmatrix}
$$

1. Calculate: $\mathbf{A}+\mathbf{B}$;  $\mathbf{B}-\mathbf{A}$;  $\mathbf{A}+\mathbf{C}$; $\mathbf{D}+\mathbf{E}$.
